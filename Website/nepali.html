The Heart of Himalayan Cooking

Nepali cuisine is a beautiful tapestry woven from the diverse geography and cultures of Nepal. From the Terai plains to the Himalayan peaks, each region contributes unique flavors and techniques.

At its core, Nepali food is about warmth, hospitality, and nourishment. The staple Dal Bhat (lentil soup with rice) is eaten twice daily by most Nepalis, while dishes like Momo and Sel Roti are beloved street foods and festival treats.

Key spices include cumin, turmeric, timur (Sichuan pepper), fenugreek, and mustard oil — giving Nepali food its distinctive, bold character.
