The Land of a Thousand Spices

Indian cuisine is one of the world's most diverse and sophisticated culinary traditions. With 28 states and 8 union territories, each region boasts its own distinct flavors, techniques, and specialties.

From the rich, creamy gravies of the North to the fiery curries of the South, from the street food of Mumbai to the royal kitchens of Lucknow — Indian food is a universe unto itself. Our recipes span this incredible spectrum.

Essential spices include garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric, cardamom, cinnamon, and the flame of Kashmiri chili.
